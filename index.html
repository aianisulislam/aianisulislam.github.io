About Me

My name is Md Anis and I’m an experienced creative visualizer, illustrator, designer and animator residing in Kolkata. I love creating soulful websites, beautiful illustrations, quirky doodles, eye catching graphics, intuitive user interface & experience, animation & motion graphics that tell a long story within a short time. If that sounds like a long list, let’s just say everything visual. I help organizations & individuals around the world by designing winning products.

Apart from the professional stuff some other words to describe me as a person are workaholic, self-motivated, self-taught and sometimes funny.
